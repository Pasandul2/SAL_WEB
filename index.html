About Us

Company Background

Silva Accessories Lanka (Pvt) Limited is a leading hygiene company specializing in manufacturing and distributing Consumer and Professional tissue and paper products for Ho.Re.Ca., AfH, and Retail markets. Our range includes napkins, toilet rolls, facial tissues, kitchen towels, and more, catering to offices, schools, hotels, healthcare, and households. We prioritize quality, affordability, and timely delivery while promoting sustainable, eco-friendly practices. With a flexible manufacturing system and strong supplier partnerships, SAL has elevated hygiene standards in Sri Lanka. Contact us today to explore our products. Our facility is located at Lot No.26A, Nalanda Ellawala Industrial Park, Kuruwita, Ratnapura.
